Cyblytics Research Group at FAST-NUCES Islamabad is dedicated to advancing the frontier of cybersecurity through cutting-edge research and

INNOVATION.

Our interdisciplinary team of researchers focuses on developing novel approaches to address the evolving challenges in cyberspace.
From threat detection and vulnerability analysis to secure communication protocols and data protection mechanisms, we strive to create impactful solutions that safeguard digital infrastructures.
